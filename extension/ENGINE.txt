Optional Engine Binary
======================

For fully self-contained engine play, place a compatible ARM Stockfish binary
at:

  /mnt/us/extensions/kindle-chess/bin/armhf/stockfish

The launcher will prefer that binary automatically.

If no local Stockfish binary is present, Kindle GlChess will fall back to:

  /mnt/us/extensions/gnomegames/bin/stockfish.sh

if that extension is installed.
